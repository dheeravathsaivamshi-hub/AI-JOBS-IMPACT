📊 AI Impact on Jobs – Power BI Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes the impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI) on jobs across various industries. It focuses on key factors such as automation risk, salary changes, productivity, and workforce adaptation.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze AI adoption across industries
Identify high-risk job roles affected by automation
Evaluate salary changes before and after AI adoption
Measure productivity and job satisfaction impact
Understand the importance of upskilling
🧩 Dashboard Structure
📄 Page 1: AI Workforce Overview
Employee distribution by industry
AI adoption levels
Automation risk analysis
Demographic insights (Gender, Education, Experience)
📄 Page 2: AI Impact & Risk Analysis
AI impact vs experience
Top 10 high-risk job roles
Productivity trends across industries
Relationship between AI impact and performance
Root cause analysis using decomposition tree
📄 Page 3: Salary & Workforce Insights
Salary comparison (Before vs After AI)
Salary growth percentage
Job satisfaction vs productivity
Remote work impact
Upskilling and workforce performance
📊 Key Insights
AI adoption is increasing across industries
Higher automation risk is observed in specific job roles
Salary growth varies based on skills and industry
Upskilling significantly improves job security and earnings
Productivity tends to improve with AI adoption
